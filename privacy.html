Privacy Policy for HQRC Studio

Last updated: June 28, 2026

HQRC Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, HQRC Studio (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

Local Processing of Images

HQRC Studio processes all images locally on your device to generate Halftone QR Codes. We do NOT upload your personal photos, logos, or generated QR codes to any remote servers. All image rendering and processing are done entirely offline on your physical device.

In-App Purchases and Billing

The App uses Google Play Billing to process subscriptions and one-time purchases (e.g., 24-Hour Pass). All payment transactions are handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your credit card or financial information.

Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information, including your device's advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID) and location data, to serve personalized ads.

For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy: Google Privacy & Terms.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing payments.
  • AdMob: For serving ads.

These third parties may collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Data Safety and Retention

Because we do not collect or store personal data or images on any servers, we do not retain your data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 3. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
